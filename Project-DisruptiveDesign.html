Market Disruption

D E S I G N   B R I E F

Shark Ninja wanted to enter a new market using the existing technology within their coffee machine . We, in a group of 5, were tasked to take the product apart, calculate its cost and possible technologies that could be used. I took the lead in disruptive design, using the technologies found in new markets to dirupt them.

The final idea consisted of using the core heating element of the coffee machine to create a modular electric heater. Using different adaptors, the heater can be turned into a camping stove or a bath water purifier (for developing countries where water purification is difficult). With additional adaptors, more functions can be acheived.
