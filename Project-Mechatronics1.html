User Centred Design

D E S I G N   B R I E F

Create an autonomous that can detect magnets hidden under sand and mark them appropriately with an end effector.

G A L L E R Y

The goal was to achieve the design brief in the faster speed possible. Hence all markers were mounted onto the end effector and magnets were marked sequentially to avoid time loss. A HALL effect sensor was used to detect magnets and create a 3D matrix on MATLAB. Data was then processed and the peak locations of HALL sensor readings were found. The stepper motors on the gantry were then autonomousely controlled to position the end effector over the magner. The end effector then deployed a market at each location.
