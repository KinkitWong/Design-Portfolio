User Centred Design

D E S I G N   B R I E F

Re-design a self service machine for a client with a specific disbility. Design the product with a user centred approach via interviews and tests with the client to ensure the product is personal and achieves its goal.

G A L L E R Y

After interviewing the client multiple times, they expressed great discomfort when useing an ATM, hence and ATM was re-designed. Using many low fidelity prototypes, the designs were tested with the user to ensure that they were comfortable using the device, taking into account ergenomic data and donald norman's 7 principles of design. The final prototype consisted of a card reader that was attached to the ATM via a retractable extention cord. The card reader was compact but was chunky enought to fit comfortably into the user's hand. Safety mechanisms were built in place to ensure the user was comfortable while using the device and vandalism was minimised.
