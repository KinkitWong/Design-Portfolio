A B O U T   M E

I am a undergraduate design engineer at the University of Bath. Ever since a young age, I have loved activities where my creativity is stretched to its limits. Once I have found a task to extend my creative thought, I become obsessed in improving my skills to complete that task to my highest capability. Tasks such as choreographing pieces for hip hop dance competitions to coding this very website. This obsessive personality of mine may sometimes be considered pedantic, however I believe this mindset is what makes me a great design engineer.
